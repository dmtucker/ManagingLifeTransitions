Joyce Freeman

 

Joyce brings to her practice as a Behavioral Therapist 30  years of experience, both in the corporate world and the private sector. Her background includes years of experience in management, training, stress - management along with endless opportunities in community services and mentoring. As a Corporate Bank Manager for 25+ years she has had the opportunity to work with and witness people dealing with all forms of  stressful situations.

 

Joyce has a Bachelor of Science degree in Psychology as well as a Paraprofessional Certification as a counselor. She is a Certified Clinical Hypnotherapist and has had additional certifications in Interactive Guided Imagery and Emotional Freedom Therapy.

 

Her professional memberships include the National Guild of Hypnotists, International Association of Interactive Imagery Guides, The National Association of Behavioral Therapists, Association for Comprehensive Energy Psychology and Business Network International. She also volunteers for Stand! Against Domestic Violence as well as The Center for AIDS Services.

 

Joyce is insightful and compassionate. Her passion is helping others by tapping into the natural ability of the mind, body and spirit connection to facilitate healing. She does this by creating an environment of relaxation and safety. Her programs assist her clients in achieving balance in their lives and help them reconnect with their "Inner Wisdom" for positive and lasting results.
