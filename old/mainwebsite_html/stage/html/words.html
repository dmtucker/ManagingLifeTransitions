Dreams

What is a dream?  A dream is a vision, a wonderful source of ideas; ideas that might not come up while one is awake. It is easy to dream, and with just a little practice and encouragement, you can close your eyes and imagine a whole new life for yourself.

You may find, that as you try to imagine this wonderful new life, you encounter some resistance… some emotional blocks. This resistance might be experienced in various ways… feelings of depression, hopelessness or even a sense of being overwhelmed. These feelings of resistance might be clues to the ways in which you avoid getting what you want in life. These blocked emotions may be keeping you from reaching your dreams.  Once acknowledged, these blocked emotions can be released from the subconscious level thru the use of hypnosis and you can then move forward.

Now…Imagine that you are experiencing your Life’s Dream…


Allow me to help you come to that place!
