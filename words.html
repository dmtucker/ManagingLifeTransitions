Stress! Is It Consuming You?
As life's demands increase, our ability to manage stress diminishes. We can become chronically stressed which affects our moods, our relationships, our motivation and ultimately our health.
You can turn stress on its ear by using the following techniques:

  • Exercise
    Take a walk during your lunch hour or afternoon break. Get out in the sunshine. Stretch!

  • Have a Positive Attitude
    Have the "Glass is half-full" rather than half-empty philosophy.

  • Be Flexible
    Learn to just BE!

  • Breathe
    Remember, deep breathing reduces tension

  • Sit Quietly, Meditate
    Take a few minutes every day just to BE!

  • Giggle
    Add humor to your life... Lighten up!
